# 👋 Hey, I’m Tristan  

I’m a **full-stack developer**, **creative technologist**, and **Computer Science student** at the **University of Reading**, currently taking a **Year in Industry** pathway.  

I build things that blend creativity and computation — from **AI-driven tutoring platforms** to **multiplayer geolocation games**, **stream automation systems**, and other projects that probably *shouldn’t work* (but do).  

🚀 Founder of **[Oakforge Studios](https://www.tristans.club/oakforgestudio)** — where code, design, and ambition collide.  
🧨 Creator of **[Missile Wars Revival](https://www.tristans.club/missilewars)** — a real-time, cross-platform, location-based strategy game.  
📚 Developer of **[Advanced Tutoring](https://advancedtutoring.uk)** — a full-stack tutoring ecosystem with payments, scheduling, AI-assisted study mode, and live video.  
🎮 Also into **Automation tools**, and experimental **AI integrations** that push what’s possible.
